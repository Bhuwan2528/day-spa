Brow & Lash Tinting

Stunning, Full & Naturally Defined Brows and Lashes

Enhance your natural beauty with professional brow and lash tinting at Day Spa at the Lakes. Our expertly customised tinting treatments add depth, definition, and fullness — creating effortlessly polished eyes without the need for daily makeup.

How This Treatment Works

Brow and lash tinting uses a specially formulated, eye-safe dye to gently enhance the natural pigment of your hair. The tint is carefully matched to complement your skin tone, hair colour, and facial features.

By darkening lighter or finer hairs, the treatment creates the appearance of fuller, thicker brows and more defined lashes — instantly enhancing eye shape and overall facial symmetry.

Unlike makeup pencils or mascara, tinting provides longer-lasting definition without smudging or daily reapplication. Results typically last between 4 to 6 weeks.

The procedure is quick, gentle, and comfortable, making it an ideal low-maintenance beauty solution for busy lifestyles.

Why Choose Our Brow & Lash Tinting?

Our experienced beauticians focus on precision shaping and colour customisation to ensure natural-looking yet noticeable results. Each treatment is tailored to enhance your features without overpowering your look.

We use high-quality, gentle products designed specifically for the delicate eye area — prioritising safety, comfort, and long-lasting definition.

What To Expect During Your Appointment

Your treatment begins with a consultation to determine the ideal shade and desired intensity. The tint is then carefully applied to ensure even coverage and precise shaping.

The process typically takes 20 to 30 minutes, making it a quick yet impactful beauty enhancement.

Aftercare guidance will be provided to help prolong your results and maintain beautifully defined brows and lashes.
