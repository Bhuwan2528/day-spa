How This Treatment Works

High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) delivers controlled ultrasound energy deep into structural layers of the skin. This energy stimulates collagen regeneration while tightening existing collagen fibres, creating gradual, natural lifting results without surgery.

Unlike surface-level treatments, HIFU works beneath the skin’s outer layers — targeting the foundational support system responsible for firmness and contour definition.

The result is improved elasticity, smoother texture and enhanced facial definition over the following weeks as collagen rebuilds naturally.
