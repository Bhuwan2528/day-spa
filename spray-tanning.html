A Gorgeous Glow All Year Round with Our Tuscan Sun Spray Tan

Achieve a flawless, sun-kissed glow at any time of the year with our premium spray tanning service. One of our most popular treatments, the Tuscan Sun spray tan is suitable for all skin tones and delivers a natural, even, and radiant finish.

At Day Spa at the Lakes, our professional tanning service is designed to enhance your natural skin tone while leaving your skin looking healthy and luminous. Whether it’s for a special occasion or everyday confidence, our spray tans provide beautiful, streak-free results.

Preparing for Your Spray Tan Appointment

To ensure you achieve the best possible results from your spray tan, we recommend following a few simple preparation steps before your appointment.

  • Gently exfoliate your skin prior to your appointment to remove dead skin cells
  • Moisturise your skin in the days leading up to your tan to maintain healthy hydration
  • Remove unwanted hair beforehand to ensure an even application of the tan
  • Avoid applying moisturiser, perfume, or deodorant on the day of your appointment

Aftercare for Long-Lasting Results

Proper aftercare helps your tan develop beautifully and last longer. Following your spray tan, we recommend keeping your first shower short and gentle.

  • Keep your first shower under one minute
  • Avoid using soap during the initial rinse
  • Pat your skin dry gently after showering
  • Continue moisturising daily to extend the life of your tan

Why Choose Our Tuscan Sun Spray Tan

Our Tuscan Sun spray tan delivers a smooth, even glow without the risks associated with sun exposure. The formula is suitable for all skin tones and provides a natural-looking finish that enhances your complexion.

Enjoy a radiant, confidence-boosting glow with our professional spray tanning service.
